Hardwares



TYPHOON HIL

Typhoon Hill specializes in advanced real-time simulation solutions for electrical engineering, offering tools for testing and optimizing power systems. Their technology enables precise modeling and analysis of complex electrical grids under various conditions.





DSPACE 1104

The dSPACE 1104 is a real-time simulation and prototyping system used for developing and testing control systems. It provides a high-performance platform for real-time simulation, hardware-in-the-loop (HIL) testing, and rapid control prototyping. The system supports various applications in automotive, aerospace, and industrial automation fields.

STM 32

STM32 refers to a series of microcontrollers developed by STMicroelectronics based on the ARM Cortex-M processor core. These microcontrollers are widely used in embedded systems for applications such as IoT, automotive, and industrial control due to their high performance, low power consumption, and extensive peripheral features.



ARDUINO NANO BLE 33 SENSE LITE

The Arduino Nano BLE 33 Sense Lite is a compact microcontroller featuring Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) for wireless communication. It includes various sensors for environmental monitoring, making it ideal for IoT projects.





HIGH-END SYSTEMS

High-end computer systems with 32 GB of RAM, a 1 TB storage drive, an Intel i9 processor, and an 8 GB GPU are designed for intensive computational tasks and data analysis. These specifications provide exceptional processing power, fast data access, and high-performance graphics, making them ideal for demanding applications and complex simulations.


OPAL-RT

The Opal RT 4500 is a real-time simulator used for hardware-in-the-loop (HIL) testing in power systems. It delivers fast, accurate simulations for validating technologies like smart grids. Its versatile interfaces ensure smooth integration with physical devices.








SERVER HPE PROLIANT

The HPE ProLiant is a series of servers from Hewlett Packard Enterprise (HPE) designed for performance, scalability, and reliability in enterprise environments. They offer a range of configurations, including rack-mounted, tower, and blade servers, and are used for various applications like data centers, cloud computing, and virtualization.
